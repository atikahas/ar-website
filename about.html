Immerse yourself in our natural surroundings with a modern classic luxury villa in Janda Baik, Pahang.

Peaceful. Tranquil. Private. Lush.

Just 50 minutes from the Kuala Lumpur city centre, Aman Rimba (which means Serene Forest in Malay) nestles on the quiet edges of Janda Baik in the district of Bentong, surrounded by abundant tropical greens and bathed by the cool hillslope air. Designed in the timeless craftsmanship of a traditional Malay “kampung” (village) along the banks of the sparkling Sungai Benus river, this private estate is perfect for both fun family weekends, and insightful, energised corporate getaways. It offers a stunning backdrop to the romance of charming weddings, and is a perfect canvas against which great ideas are born. A variety of entertainment and leisure activities that draw from the communal village living of yesteryears makes it a perfect setting for reunions of friends and families. It has six private villas spread over three undulating acres, and can comfortably hold 20 pax, and up to 35 with rollaway beds, in thoughtfully-designed private rooms. Aman Rimba’s well-trained and discreet staff see to all its guests’ needs around the clock, while a team of village chefs offer a variety of beautifully prepared Malay dishes made from the freshest ingredients. Don’t forget to pre-arrange your meals when you make your booking so that we can ensure a meal you won’t forget. Best of all, a stay at Aman Rimba means having the entire estate to yourself; no one but your guests will have access to the estate during that time. Take a step back in time, let the beauty of nature soothe your soul and inspire your mind. And enjoy the intimacy of being in this very private estate.

Just 50 mins from KL, Aman Rimba offers a quick escape from the stresses of the city.
At 415m above sea level, Aman Rimba lets you enjoy the cool mountain air, and revel in the distant whispers of the lush tropical forest.
Rest amidst Aman Rimba’s beautiful gardens. Hear the happy chirps of birds, and sometimes, even the calls of gibbons!
Pandan Hall is the nexus of Aman Rimba. Enjoy hot beverages 24/7, while meal times are literally a feast for the eyes and tastebuds.
Aman Rimba’s team of village chefs will take you back to the time when food was meant to be savoured. Don’t forget to pre-order your meals!
Enjoy the fruits of Aman Rimba’s orchards during fruiting season — durians, rambutans, mata kucing, buah terap, langsat, machang, mangosteens, jackfruit and cempedak.
