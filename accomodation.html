The Villas

Perfect place for family gateways or vacations with friends.

DOWNLOAD PDF

Inspired by the charming local villages of yesteryears, Aman Rimba is a wonderful blend of authentic traditional architecture and fine, simple luxury. Our villas are airy and bright, with large windows that invite the outdoors in. All 6 villas offer a garden setting, and each villa features all the necessary conveniences – comfortable beds made for peaceful sleep-ins, ample living areas within the rooms where you can read and relax, and a quiet verandah to while away the evening.

All villas include charming ensuite hot-water bathrooms that let the light in and air-conditioning if you so desire. The villas are designed to comfortably sleep 20; if you have a larger group, please speak to our manager when you make your booking about providing rollaway beds. We are able, with advance notice, to accommodate up to 35 pax when needed, provided prior arrangements are made.

Common areas are within easy reach of all villas and wireless broadband is available for those who must absolutely stay in touch with the virtual world, yet the lush vegetation and dense equatorial greens make sure you’re cocooned in peace and tranquility.
