COVID-19 Statement

We are taking robust measures to ensure that the wellbeing of our guests and employees remain our highest priority.

We are closely monitoring the official advise by both the World Health Organization and the guidelines from Ministry of Health Malaysia (KKM) on the steps we are undertaking.

We firmly believe this needs to be a joint effort, so we ask you to please play your part in keeping everyone safe.

SCAPES SAFETY - Our steps in keeping everyone safe!.

Scapes Safety Steps:-

Contact Tracing:
Self-Declaration through MySejahtera for every entry into Aman Rimba Private Estate.


Temperature Scan:
Daily temperature check upon entering.


Face Mask:
Face Mask are required to be worn at all times.


Hand Wash or Sanitizing:
Wash your hands with soap and water, and dry them thoroughly or sanitise your hands with sanitizers provided in public areas.


Social Distancing:
Social distancing of 1 metre in all areas.


Frequency of Cleaning:
High frequency cleaning & disinfecting in public areas at all times.
