The Aman Rimba Private Estate in Janda Baik, Pahang is designed in the timeless craftsmanship of a traditional Malay “kampung” (village) along the banks of sparkling Sungai Benus river, this private estate is perfect for both fun family weekends, and insightful, energised corporate getaways.
