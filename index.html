About Us

Peaceful. Tranquil. Private. Lush.

The Aman Rimba Private Estate in Janda Baik, Pahang is designed in the timeless craftsmanship of a traditional Malay “kampung” (village) along the banks of the sparkling Sungai Benus river, this private estate is perfect for both fun family weekends, and insightful, energised corporate getaways. Take a step back in time, let the beauty of nature soothe your soul and inspire your mind. Enjoy the intimacy of being in this very private estate.
